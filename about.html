Bringing Freshness to Your Community
Since 2024

At Grocies , we're more than just a place to buy groceries; we're a community hub where you can find everything you need for a healthy and delicious life. Founded in 2024, we've been serving the communities of Havelock City, Galle face, and Colombo with fresh, high-quality products and friendly service for over 1 year.

Three Locations, One Promise

We have three conveniently located stores in Havelock City, Galle Face, and Colombo, each offering the same commitment to freshness, quality, and customer service. No matter which location you visit, you can expect to find a welcoming atmosphere and a wide selection of products to meet your needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with a convenient and enjoyable shopping experience, offering a wide variety of high-quality groceries at competitive prices. We believe in supporting local farmers and producers whenever possible, bringing fresh, local produce to your table. Additionally, we strive to offer a diverse selection of national brands and international products to cater to all your dietary sneeds and preferences.

Our Commitment to You

  • Convenient Shopping:
    We offer various shopping options to suit your needs, including in-store shopping, online ordering with pickup or delivery, and weekly flyers with special offers.
  • Community Focus:
    We are committed to supporting local communities through partnerships with local farms and charities.
  • Customer Service:
    Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always here to assist you and answer any questions you may have.
  • Freshness First:
    We prioritize freshness in all our departments, from crisp fruits and vegetables to high-quality meats and dairy products.
