Dive into the Vibrant World of Fresh Vegetables at Grocies !

At Grocies , we believe fresh, high-quality vegetables are the foundation of a healthy and delicious diet. That's why we source our produce from the best farms around the world, ensuring you get the freshest, most flavorful vegetables all year round.

Explore Our Colorful Selection

From seasonal favorites to everyday staples, we offer a wide variety of vegetables to suit every recipe and taste. Browse through our aisles and discover.

  • Crisp lettuces, juicy tomatoes, and vibrant bell peppers:
    Perfect for adding freshness and flavor to salads and sandwiches.
  • Nutrient-packed broccoli, spinach, and kale:
    These leafy greens are loaded with vitamins and minerals.
  • Hearty root vegetables like carrots, potatoes, and beets:
    Ideal for roasting, mashing, or adding to stews and soups.
  • Exotic mushrooms, colorful peppers, and unique Asian greens:
    Explore new flavors and add excitement to your dishes.

Beyond the Basics

We understand that some of you might be looking for specific or hard-to-find vegetables. Don't hesitate to ask our knowledgeable staff; they're happy to help you locate specialty items or answer any questions you may have. We work closely with local farmers who prioritize sustainable practices and freshness. Our dedicated produce team carefully selects each vegetable to ensure you get the highest quality produce at peak ripeness. Look for the "local hero" signs highlighting fresh produce sourced directly from Sri Lankan farms!

Beyond the Supermarket Aisles

To ensure our customers receive the best vegetables, We collect vegetables daily from the best suppliers in the country. Our main suppliers are listed below.

  1. Peliyagoda Wholesale Market:
    A bustling hub for fresh produce from across the country.
  2. Farmers' Markets:
    These community markets often feature seasonal vegetables directly from local farms.
  3. "Meega Elle" Organic Farms:
    Offers certified organic vegetables delivered to your doorstep.
  4. "Hela" Agri Products:
    Find a curated selection of Sri Lankan grown vegetables alongside other local products.
  5. "Farm Fresh" Online Market:
    Browse and order fresh, seasonal vegetables delivered straight from Sri Lankan farms.
